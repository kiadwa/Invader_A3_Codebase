Instruction to Play the game
**To run: "gradle run" command
**To move player: Left & Right arrow key
**To shoot: Space Bar

**To Save the game: Press "S" after making a shot
**To restore: Press "R" after saving

**To Cheat: "Digit 4" to remove fast projectile
            "Digit 5" to remove slow projectile
            "Digit 6" to remove slow aliens
            "Digit 7" to remove fast aliens
